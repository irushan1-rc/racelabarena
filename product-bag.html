About This Bag

Lightweight, rugged and built for the driven. Whether you're heading to the track, the gym, or the weekend away — the RLA Stay Driven Duffel keeps your gear organised and your look on point. Clean white canvas, bold RLA branding, and contrasting black straps. This isn't just a bag — it's a statement.

A roomy main compartment handles everything from clothes to car parts, while an internal zip pocket and exterior mesh pocket keep the small stuff reachable. The adjustable padded shoulder strap and reinforced handles make it comfortable even when fully loaded. Assembled right here in AU.

What's Inside the Design
  • 100% polyester Oxford canvas — durable and quick-drying
  • Zippered main compartment with black double zipper
  • Internal zip pocket with sewn-in name tag
  • Exterior mesh side pocket for quick-access items
  • Removable, adjustable 1.5" shoulder strap with pad + brushed nickel hardware
  • Reinforced carrying handles for heavier loads
  • Available in Small and Large — assembled in AU (±0.5" tolerance)
