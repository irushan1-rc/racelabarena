The Experience

Premium Technology Meets Real Racing

State-of-the-art equipment and custom-built systems deliver an unmatched experience

⚙️

Simulator Steering Wheel

Professional racing wheel with precision control — your inputs go directly to the real car.

📡

Real-Time FPV Camera

Live onboard 1080p camera feed puts you in the driver's seat with minimal latency.

📈

Live Telemetry Dashboard

Real-time speed, steering angle, throttle, and performance data shown on screen.

⚡

Low-Latency Response

Custom-built wireless bridge delivers instant control for an authentic feel.

🔧

Custom Tuning Modes

Adjust difficulty, speed limits, and handling characteristics to suit your level.

🏆

Lap Timer & Leaderboard

Every lap is recorded. Chase the fastest time and compete with others.
