🎮 The Experience
RaceLab Arena is Perth's first real RC racing experience. You sit behind a Logitech G29 sim racing wheel, look through a live FPV camera feed, and control a real RC car on a real track. It's not a simulation — the car is real, the speed is real, and the experience is unlike anything else.
The FPV camera streams live 1080p HD video at low latency directly to a screen in front of you, putting you in the driver's seat of the car. You see exactly what the car sees in real time.
We use the FS Racing Rebel SC V2 Short Course Truck — a high-performance RC car built for speed and durability. It's powered by a 3S LiPo battery and runs on a custom-built wireless control bridge developed specifically for RLA.
Yes. The RC car operates within a controlled track area. All electrical systems are custom designed and tested for safety. The car is small enough to be fully controlled at all times.
